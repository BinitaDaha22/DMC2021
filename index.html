Introduction

Damak Multiple Campus was established in 1981 AD (2038 BS) as a community owned Campus; which is located in Damak-9, Jhapa of Nepal. It is only the QAA certified campus in the Eastern Nepal, affiliated to Tribhuvan University and National Examinations Board (NEB). It is completely supported and managed by the local people of Damak. It emphasis on management gradually, the focus has gone to all faculties- Humanities, Education, Management and Science. The Environment Science programs has drawn much attention due to its global issues. Master degree programs is also an excellent for the production of qualified manpower. It equally has given importance to extracurricular activities to boost the studentâ€™s genius. The students are permitted to launch programs through the literary forum, Youth Red Cross Circle (YRCC) and so on. Damak Multiple Campus offers TU affiliated programs such as Bachelor of Arts (BA), Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS), Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.), Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Environmental Science, Bachelor of Science (B.Sc.) in Physics and Master of Business Studies (MBS). It also offers NEB affiliated ten plus two programs such as Education, Management, Humanities and Science.
